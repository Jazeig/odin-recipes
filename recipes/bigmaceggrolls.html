BigMac Egg Rolls!

DESCRIPTION

BigMac Egg Rolls the best way to enjoy all the great flavors of a classic Big Mac but without the heavy bun and additives of a regular Big Mac! taste just like a Big Mac!

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Pickle
  • Onion
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Special Sauce - as simple as mixing together classic condiments - mayo, mustard, ketchup and relish with a splash of apple cider vinegar

STEPS

  1. In a medium sized frying pan cook the ground beef until fully cooked, approximately 10 minutes. Drain the meat. Season with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  2. To assemble the egg rolls place one slice of cheese in the center. Top with 2-3 spoonfuls of ground beef, a few pieces of diced pickle and onion.
  3. Wet the edges of the egg roll wrapper. Fold in two opposite corners to the center and then tightly roll. Repeat with remaining egg roll wrappers.
  4. Cook egg rolls in preferred method: Air fry/Deep fry/Oven
